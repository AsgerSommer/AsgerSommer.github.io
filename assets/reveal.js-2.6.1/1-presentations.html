How I make my presentations

Using reveal.js

Created by Asger Sommer / @Sommer717

USE REVEAL.JS

Press ESC to enter the slide overview.

Press B to enter the pause mode.

Don't use vertical slides.

USE BETTER SLIDES

Each slide should have a singular purpose.

The fewer slides the better.

The simpler slides the better.

USE A REFRAIN

A mantra/point that you repeat.

Something that captures the entire point of the talk.

PRACTICE. PRACTICE. PRACTICE.

Practice in front of friends.

Record yourself practicing.
